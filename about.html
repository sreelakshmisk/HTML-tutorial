HTML FORMATTING

Break tag

"br" is used as break tag.
It is used for breaking lines.

Bold tag

"b" is used as bold tag. It is used to make text,phrases and words are bold .

Strong tag

Strong tag is used for strong text. Text,phrase or words with strong importance are displayed as bold.

Italic tag

"i" is used as italic tag. It is used for italic text.

Emphasized tag

"em" is used for emphasized text. It emphasize a word/phrase or text,displayed in italic.

Mark tag

"mark" tag is used for highlight a word,phrase or text.

Small tag

"small" tag is used to make a phrase or text small.
